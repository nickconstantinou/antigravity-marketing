# Skill 07: Recurring Revenue Newsletter
- **Structure:** 1 Big Idea -> 3 Curated Links -> 1 Soft CTA.
- **The "9 Formats" Rule:** Rotate between "The Case Study," "The Contrarian Take," and "The Tool Breakdown" to avoid audience fatigue.
- **Protocol:** Subject lines must be 20% "Open Loop" (mystery) and 80% "Payoff."
